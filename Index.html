About Me

My name is Sammy Cullinan and I am a Full Stack Web Developer. I graduated from Arizona State University in 2019 with a degree in Biochemistry. Throughout college I always had an interest in programming languages and would occasionally try to teach myself. Following graduation and a few attempts at learning coding I realized that I thoroughly enjoyed coding. It was then I decided to pursue a career in programming and web development. I was accepted into the University of Arizona's Coding Bootcamp where I learned full-stack development and completed the course in November 2020. I am knowledgable in HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, JavaScript, ES6, jQuery, AJAX, CDN, Node.js, Express.js, MySQL, MySQLite, MongoDB, Mongoose, React, GraphQL, and Git. I am also familiar with Test-Driven-Development (TDD), Object-Oriented Programming (OOP), Object Relational Mapping (ORM), and Model-View-Controller (MVC).
